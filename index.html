About


Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Kevin Systrom and I hail from Holliston in USA. I founded the world's largest photo sharing website along with my pal Mike Krieger. I graduated in 2006 with a bachelor's degree in Management Science and Engineering from Standford University. After graduation I joined Google, working on Gmail, Google Calendar, Docs, Spreadsheets and other products. In 2010, I founded the photo sharing, later, video sharing social networking service Instagram.

Instagram was originally distiguished by allowing content to be framed only in a square(1:1) aspect ratio. In 2015, the restriction was eased to upload multiple aspect ratios. It also added messaging features, the ability to include multiple images or videos in a single post and stories feature. It become massive hit around the globe and by January 2019, Stories were is used by 500 million people daily.
